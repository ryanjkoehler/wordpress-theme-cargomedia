Best of QCon London

Posted on March 19, 2013 by Reto | Posted in Culture | Tagged conference, qcon | No Comments

We attended this year’s QCon London conference which featured lots of interesting talks in tracks like “Startups”, “Mobile banking”, “Architecture” or “Big Data Nosql”. Our stay in London was wonderful, we were accommodated in a spacious apartment in Shoreditch with short distances to some great pubs, shops etc!

Only for you guys we compiled the definitive “Best of Talks”! Go have a look and tell us what you think.
(To access the “Video”-links you’ll need the access token, which you received via email if you’ve attended the conference.)

  • Developing the FT web app (Robert Shilston): Slides, Video

See you next time in London!
